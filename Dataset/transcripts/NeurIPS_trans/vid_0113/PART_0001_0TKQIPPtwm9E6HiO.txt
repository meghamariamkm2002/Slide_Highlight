The COVID-19 pandemic continues to bring up various topics discussed or debated on social media.
In order to explore the impact of pandemics on people's lives, it is important to understand the public's concerns and attitudes towards pandemic-related entities on social media.
However, most NLP tools as well as models trained on existing name entity recognition or targeted sentiment analysis data sets have limited ability to understand COVID-19 related social media texts because these data sets are not designed or annotated from a medical perspective.
